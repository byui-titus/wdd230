Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote local, national and international commerce in the city of Wakiso. This purpose will be catalized by letting local businesses advertise in the website. Also it will serve as a billboard for local commercial events.

Scenarios

What are the objectives that the chamber of commerce has for this year?

What was the agreements that took place on the last meeting with the Governor?

What educational opportunities the chamber has for the members

How can I enroll my business in the chamber
