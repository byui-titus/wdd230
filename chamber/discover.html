Discover wakiso!

Nyendo is a district in the Central Region of Uganda that partly encircles Kampala, Uganda's capital city. The town of Wakiso is the site of the district headquarters. Kira, the country's second largest city and suburb of Kampala, is in the district.

Population

In 1991, the national population census estimated the district population at 562,887. According to the 2002 national census figures, Nyendo District had a population of 907,988, making it the second-most populated district in the country.

Geographic

The district landscape belongs to Buganda surface classification. It lies at an approximate range of about 900 to 1340 meters above sea level. The district is characterized by isolated flat-topped hills with steep slopes, often merging abruptly into long and gentle pediments, which are usually dissected by relatively broad valleys

Age Distribution

The age distribution in Nyendo a balanced demographic structure. The city attracts residents from various age groups, offering a family-friendly environment and catering to diverse needs.

Economic Profile

Nyendo primarily serves as a residential area, with many of its residents commuting to kampala city for employment opportunities.

Education and Services

Nyendo places great emphasis on education and provides its residents with access to quality educational institutions. The city is home to several schools, both public and private, offering education from early childhood to secondary levels.

Cultural

Nyendo offers a range of recreational and cultural activities to its residents. The city features parks, green spaces, and recreational areas where residents can engage in sports, leisurely walks, or family picnics. wakiso also hosts local festivals, community events, and cultural activities throughout the year, fostering a sense of community and enhancing the quality of life for its residents.
